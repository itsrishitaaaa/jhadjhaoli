💆 Aloe Vera Hair Mask

Frizz? Dry scalp? Dull strands? This aloe mask will revive your hair with moisture and shine, naturally.

🛒 Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp fresh aloe vera gel
  • 1 tbsp coconut oil or castor oil
  • Few drops of lemon juice (optional)

🧴 Instructions:

  1. Mix all ingredients into a smooth paste.
  2. Apply from scalp to ends.
  3. Cover with a shower cap and leave for 30 mins.
  4. Wash with mild shampoo and air dry.
