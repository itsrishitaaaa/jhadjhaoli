🧖 Neem Face Pack for Glowing Skin

Say goodbye to pimples and dullness with this powerful neem face mask — an all-natural skin detox.

🛒 Ingredients:

  • Fresh neem leaves or neem powder
  • Rose water or plain water
  • 1 tsp turmeric (optional)

🧴 Instructions:

  1. Grind neem leaves into a fine paste.
  2. Mix with rose water and turmeric.
  3. Apply evenly on the face.
  4. Leave for 15 minutes, then rinse with cool water.
