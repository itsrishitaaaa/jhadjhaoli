Plant Care Tips

Water Wisely

Water your plants early in the morning and only when the topsoil feels dry.

Sunlight Needs

Different plants need different levels of sunlight. Avoid direct afternoon sun for delicate ones.

Soil Health

Use organic compost or natural fertilizers to enrich soil quality and retain moisture.

Pruning & Grooming

Trim dead leaves to encourage new growth and keep your plant looking neat.

Right Pot Size

Make sure your plant has enough space. Repot annually for better growth.

Watch for Pests

Use neem oil spray or mild soap water to treat common pests naturally.
